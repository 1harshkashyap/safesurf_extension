Fake Login Pages — #1 Credential Threat
Attackers clone login pages of banks, email, and social networks pixel-by-pixel. SafeSurf v5 detects these by simultaneously analysing domain legitimacy, password fields, brand imagery, and login language — flagging what a URL check alone misses.
🧬
Adaptive Trust — SafeSurf Learns Your Behaviour
Every site you visit safely is remembered. After 2+ safe visits, SafeSurf increases trust confidence for that domain. After 10+ visits, it provides a significant trust boost. This is how the system becomes personalised to your browsing patterns over time.
🌐
Domain Reputation Intelligence (NEW)
SafeSurf v5 checks every domain against a multi-layer reputation database: trusted sites, financial institutions, government domains, suspicious TLDs, and free hosting platforms. This adds an external intelligence signal to the analysis.
🧠
Behavioral Anomaly Detection (NEW)
Beyond static analysis, SafeSurf v5 detects behavioral patterns: auto-focused password fields, popup login traps, clipboard hijacking, exit prevention, and hidden iframes. These are real-time signals that static scanners miss entirely.
⚗️
Attack Simulation — See the Actual Threat
SafeSurf's Attack Simulation panel shows exactly what would happen if a detected risk were exploited — what data could be stolen, what the consequences are, and how difficult the attack is.
🔗
URL Anatomy — Reading the Real Domain
In "paypal.secure-verify.com" the actual domain is "secure-verify.com" — not PayPal. The real domain is always the part immediately before the TLD (.com/.net/etc.).
